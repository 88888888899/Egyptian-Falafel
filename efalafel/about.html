Ta'ameya ( Egyptian Falafel )

Falafel or ta'ameya as we call it in Egypt,
is an all-time favourite street food. In most
parts of the Middle East, falafel is made with
ground beans. This is not your traditional recipe,
this one contains three different of aromantic herbs
as well as 5 spices.
The ingredients for Egyptian Ta'ameya are carefully
selected and finely combined.
