Ta'ameya ( Egyptian Falafel )

Falafel or ta'ameya as we call it in Egypt, is an all-time favourite
street food. In most parts of the Middle East, falafel is made with
ground beans. This is not your traditional Falafel recipe, this one
contains three different of aromantic herbs as well as 5 spices.
This wonderful falafel recieves much love from all natiounalities.
